Trust and privacy

Designed to earn trust before asking for more.

AshTag is built to remain usable without an account and without turning every permission into a requirement. The app stays device-first unless the user chooses otherwise.

No account required

The core experience works without sign-up or a personal account.

Optional permissions

Location, notifications, Health, and Motion & Fitness are optional, not required for basic use.

Optional Cloud Sync

Users can keep data on one device or enable Cloud Sync across signed Apple devices on the same iCloud account.

Data control in the app

Export, import, delete, and reset tools are available inside the product.

Product stance

AshTag is designed to feel calm, not preachy.

It is not built to shame people into perfect streaks. It is not trying to sound like clinical treatment. It is not here to turn every tough day into failure.

AshTag is for logging honestly, using support quickly, and noticing patterns clearly enough to make the next decision with a little more room.
