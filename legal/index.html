Legal Notes

AshTag supports self-management. It is not medical care or emergency support.

AshTag may show supportive suggestions, reminders, hotspot insights, and rescue tools, but it is not a substitute for medical care, diagnosis, or urgent crisis help.

What AshTag is

AshTag is a self-management and habit-awareness companion for people trying to quit smoking or understand smoking patterns more clearly.

What AshTag is not

  • Not medical care
  • Not a diagnosis tool
  • Not emergency support

If urgent help is needed

  • Contact local emergency services when appropriate.
  • Use immediate smoking-cessation support such as the National Quitline when needed.

Questions about current scope

If you need help understanding what AshTag does in the current release, use the public support page or review the privacy notes for permission and data-handling detail.
